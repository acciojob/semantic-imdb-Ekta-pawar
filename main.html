Summary

Inception is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious while they dream. When offered a chance to erase his criminal history, he embarks on a mission to implant an idea instead—an act known as "inception."

Main Actors

  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
